Creche

The Montessori section of SwotRoyal School is a well equipped creche environment for laying a well-rounded foundation for children aged 18months to 5years+. The curriculum has been designed to help a child unfold her/his talents, to enable and guide him/her to sharpen her/his mental and physical abilities. Rhymes, Stories, Pictorial Play, writing aspect and Literature, Picture reading


Nursery

Our Nursery school is well-designed to inspire and nurture children in a child-centred environment to aid your child's education and development.

The curriculum for the Nursery section include:

  • Reading Readiness
  • Number readiness
  • Elementary Science
  • Writing
  • Drawing and Painting
  • Rhymes
  • Social Studies
  • Story telling
  • physical exercises
  • Religious knowledge
  • Computer knowledge

Primary School

The Primary Formal School is set to establish a fundamental and effective skill level in reading, writing and oral communication to further learning.

The curriculum for the Primary section include:

  • English Language (Comprehension, Grammar, Literature-in-English, Composition, Phonetics
  • Mathematics
  • Elementary Science
  • Vocational Aptitude
  • Health Education
  • French Language
  • Social Studies
  • Indigenous Language
  • Creative Arts
  • Religious knowledge
  • Computer knowledge
  • Quantitative Reasoning
  • Verbal Reasoning
  • Music
  • Agric Science
  • Home Economics
