From the Proprietress Desk

Dear Parents and Students,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to our School website. As Proprietress I want to reassure you of our commitment to the school and the provision of an excellent all-round education for our students in our state of the art facility.

In the uncertain times of the 2020-2021 school year, there are two things we know for certain:

1) This year will be different than any school year in the history of our school and,

2) Our families and students can still count on Swotroyal Montessori School as being a place of learning and support!

School may look different this year with online learning and, hopefully, at some point returning to an in-building hybrid model.
Yet what will never change is our commitment to serving our students community with knowledge, compassion, and dedication.

Academic learning and growth are our bottom line, yet we also understand that educating students means being attentive to the “whole child” and caring about their social and emotional well-being. Swotroyal Montessori School is a place of learning, belonging, growth, and support for students, families, and community.
Thank you for being a part of our community and school!
Let us know how we can help make your experience even better.
We look forward to hearing from you!

Sincerely,
Proprietress, Swotroyal Montessori School
